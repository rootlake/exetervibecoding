Breakout debrief — facilitation prompts (10–15 min)

WORKED
- What came together in under 10 minutes?
- Which tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) felt best for your prompt? Why?
- Did anyone get something they'd actually use next week?

BROKE
- Where did the AI misunderstand your intent?
- What did you have to rewrite by hand?
- Did layout, colors, or interactivity fail?

SURPRISED
- Anything better than you expected?
- Any tool do something the others couldn't?
- Ethics or fairness concerns come up?

BRIDGE TO CURSOR
- "Canvas gave you a one-file draft. Cursor lets you split HTML + data + iterate across a whole folder."
